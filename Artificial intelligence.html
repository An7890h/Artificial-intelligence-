Artificial Intelligence in Education

English
العربية

Abstract

Artificial Intelligence is transforming education through personalized learning, intelligent tutoring systems, and automated administration. This research examines how AI technologies enhance educational outcomes by adapting to individual learning styles, providing real-time feedback, and reducing teacher workload. Key applications include adaptive learning platforms that customize content delivery, AI-powered assessment tools for objective evaluation, and virtual assistants that support both students and educators. The study also addresses challenges such as data privacy concerns, algorithmic bias, and the need for human oversight in AI-driven education. By analyzing current implementations and future trends, this paper provides a framework for integrating AI in educational settings while maintaining pedagogical integrity and equity. The findings suggest that when implemented thoughtfully, AI can significantly improve access to quality education, optimize learning processes, and prepare students for a technology-driven future.
